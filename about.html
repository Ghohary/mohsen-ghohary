About Mohsen Ghohary

Redefining Luxury Through Timeless Design

Our Story

Founded with a vision to revolutionize luxury fashion, Mohsen Ghohary represents the pinnacle of sophistication and craftsmanship. Our journey began with a simple belief: that true luxury lies not in excess, but in the perfect marriage of elegance, quality, and innovation.

Every piece in our collection is meticulously designed and crafted by master artisans who understand that luxury is an experience, not just a possession. We combine traditional techniques with contemporary vision to create garments and accessories that transcend trends and define timeless style.

At Mohsen Ghohary, we don't just create fashion—we create art that celebrates the beauty of refinement and the power of self-expression through impeccable design.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional luxury collections that inspire confidence, celebrate individuality, and stand the test of time. We are committed to creating pieces that seamlessly blend heritage craftsmanship with cutting-edge design, ensuring every customer experiences the true meaning of luxury.

Our Core Values

Craftsmanship

Every stitch, every seam, every detail is executed with precision and passion. Our artisans bring decades of expertise to create pieces of uncompromising quality.

Innovation

We blend tradition with modernity, constantly exploring new techniques and materials to push the boundaries of luxury fashion while respecting timeless elegance.

Sustainability

We believe luxury and responsibility go hand in hand. Our commitment to ethical practices and sustainable materials ensures beauty that lasts for generations.

Excellence

We never compromise. From raw materials to final delivery, every aspect meets our exacting standards of excellence and quality.

Heritage

We honor the rich traditions of haute couture while creating the luxury pieces of tomorrow. Our designs are rooted in history and vision.

Exclusivity

Limited collections and bespoke services ensure that each piece is as unique as the individual who wears it. Luxury means being one of few, not one of many.

Our Journey

2015

Mohsen Ghohary founded with a vision to create luxury fashion that transcends time and trends.

2017

Launched our first collection of haute couture dresses and signature accessories. Recognition from international fashion critics.

2019

Expanded to include premium leather goods and expanded our global presence across major luxury markets.

2021

Introduced our sustainable luxury initiative, committing to ethical sourcing and eco-friendly production methods.

2023

Launched our bespoke customization service, allowing clients to create truly personalized luxury pieces.

2025

Today, Mohsen Ghohary is recognized globally as a leader in contemporary luxury fashion and timeless elegance.
